Privacy Policy

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Overview

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director is designed as a local tournament management application for home poker leagues and Texas Holdem tournaments. The app helps users create, run, save, and review poker tournament activity while keeping tournament data under the control of the person operating the app.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be stored, how it is used, when it may leave the device, and what choices users have. This policy applies to the CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director application and this supporting website.

Information Stored By The App

The application may store tournament-related information locally on the device where the app is installed. This may include:

  • Player names, nicknames, emails, phone numbers, and notes, if entered by the user
  • League, season, and game type values
  • Tournament names, descriptions, dates, notes, and setup details
  • Player registration, seating, table assignments, finish positions, and results
  • Blind structures, round timing, break timing, antes, and lockout settings
  • Buy-ins, rebuys, add-ons, rake, bounty settings, chip amounts, and prize payouts
  • Chip inventory, chip colors, chip values, and chip quantities
  • Recorded tournament actions such as rebuys, add-ons, bust outs, undo actions, and receipts
  • League standings, player statistics, cashes, wins, knockouts, points, and net results
  • Application settings such as theme, receipt options, display preferences, and sound choices

The app does not require users to create a CoreNucApps account to use these local tournament management features.

How Information Is Used

Information stored by the application is used to provide tournament management features, including:

  • Creating and saving tournament setups
  • Managing player registration, seating, rebuys, add-ons, bust outs, and receipts
  • Running the tournament clock and tracking blind rounds
  • Calculating prize pools, payouts, points, standings, and player net results
  • Showing tournament details on the main display and optional TV browser display
  • Exporting reports, backups, standings, receipts, or settlement information when requested by the user
  • Remembering local app preferences and display settings

CoreNucApps does not use locally stored tournament data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Local Storage And Backups

Tournament data is stored locally by the application, including data stored in the app database and local settings. Users may choose to export a database backup or import a database backup through the app's settings.

Exported files are controlled by the user. If a user saves, copies, emails, uploads, or otherwise shares an exported file, that file may contain tournament and player information from the app.

Device-level backup services, cloud sync tools, antivirus tools, or file sharing services controlled by the user or operating system may also process files stored on the device. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies and settings.

Sharing And Export Features

The app may allow users to create or share player receipts, standings, settlement reports, database backups, or other tournament-related output. Sharing happens only when the user chooses to use an export or share feature.

When using operating system share features, the selected destination, such as email, messaging, cloud storage, or another app, may receive the shared content. Users should review the information before sharing it and should only share it with people or services they trust.

TV Browser Display And Local Network Use

The desktop app may include a TV Browser Display feature. When started by the user, this feature publishes selected tournament display information from the host computer to a lightweight local web page on the user's home network.

The TV Browser Display is intended for use on trusted devices connected to the same local network. Anyone who can access the local display URL while the server is running may be able to view the selected tournament information shown by that feature.

The user can stop the TV Browser Display from within the app. The display should not be used on untrusted public networks.

Information We Do Not Collect

CoreNucApps does not intentionally collect the following through the app:

  • Payment card numbers or bank account information
  • Government identification numbers
  • Precise device location
  • Contacts, photos, camera content, or microphone recordings
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising

Users should avoid entering sensitive personal information into tournament notes or player records unless they are comfortable storing that information locally in the app.

Website Privacy

This supporting website is a static informational website. It provides product information, help content, screenshots, technology details, and this Privacy Policy.

The website does not include account sign-in, payment processing, comments, or forms that intentionally collect personal information. If the site is hosted by a third-party platform, that platform may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, request logs, and security telemetry according to its own policies.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on operating system services or user-selected apps for actions such as sharing, opening files, saving files, printing, backups, updates, or app store distribution. Those services are provided by the operating system, device vendor, app store, or selected destination app.

CoreNucApps is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services that users choose to use with exported or shared tournament information.

Data Retention And Deletion

Tournament data remains in the app until the user deletes it, resets it, imports over it, removes the app, clears app data, or otherwise deletes the underlying files from the device.

Users can manage data from within the app where supported, including editing or deleting players, games, setup records, and related tournament information. Exported backups and shared files must be managed separately by the user in the location where those files were saved or sent.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to help protect app data through ordinary platform storage mechanisms and user-controlled device security. Users are encouraged to protect their devices with strong sign-in options, keep operating systems up to date, and be careful when exporting or sharing tournament information.

No software, local database, network display, backup file, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

User Choices

Users control what information they enter into the app and how that information is used. Users can:

  • Choose whether to enter optional player contact details or notes
  • Edit or remove local tournament setup records where supported by the app
  • Choose whether to export backups, reports, receipts, or standings
  • Choose whether to start or stop the TV Browser Display
  • Delete exported files from the locations where they were saved
  • Uninstall the app or clear app data according to the device's operating system

Children's Privacy

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director is intended for adults who organize poker tournaments and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. CoreNucApps does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app or website.

International Users

The app is intended to store tournament information locally on the user's device. If users export, back up, or share data through third-party services, that information may be processed in locations determined by those services.
