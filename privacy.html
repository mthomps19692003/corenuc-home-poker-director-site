Privacy Policy

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director

Introduction

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and stored while using the application.

Information Collection

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director is designed primarily as a local tournament management application for home poker leagues and Texas Holdem tournaments.

The application may store tournament-related information locally on your device, including:

  • Player names
  • Tournament configurations
  • Blind structures
  • Buy-in and payout information
  • League standings and tournament history

This information is used solely to provide tournament management functionality.

Data Usage

Information stored by the application is used only for:

  • Managing poker tournaments
  • Tracking tournament progress
  • Saving tournament history and league statistics
  • Improving application functionality and user experience

Data Sharing

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Tournament and player information remains under the control of the user operating the application unless the user chooses to export, share, or distribute that information independently.

Internet Connectivity

Some application features may require internet access for updates, licensing, streaming, or related services. Any such connections are used only to support application functionality.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to help protect locally stored information. However, no software or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

CoreNuc Home Poker Tournament Director is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
