About the App

CoreNuc Poker League Manager is built for home poker hosts, league organizers, and tournament directors who need a simple way to manage Texas Holdem tournaments. The app helps keep games organized by tracking players, tables, blind rounds, buy-ins, rebuys, add-ons, payouts, and tournament progress from one central application.

Whether you are running a casual poker night, a recurring league event, or a structured multi-table tournament, this application provides the tools needed to keep the tournament moving smoothly and professionally.

Key Features

Tournament Setup

Create and configure Texas Holdem tournaments with blind levels, starting chips, round times, breaks, and payout structures.

Player Management

Track registered players, seating assignments, table movement, eliminations, and tournament standings.

Buy-ins and Add-ons

Manage buy-ins, rebuys, add-ons, prize pools, and player payments during live tournament play.

Live Tournament Clock

Display current blinds, upcoming blinds, timers, breaks, and tournament status for players and organizers.

League Tracking

Maintain organized tournament history, player results, and league standings across multiple events.

Easy to Use

A clean interface helps home game hosts run organized tournaments without unnecessary complexity.

Who It Is For

  • Home poker leagues
  • Texas Holdem tournament hosts
  • Recurring poker night organizers
  • Charity or private poker tournament directors
  • Players who want a more professional tournament experience
